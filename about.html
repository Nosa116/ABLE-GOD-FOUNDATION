About Us

Welcome to Able God Foundation Established Since 2005

The Able God Educational & Skills Empowerment Foundation is a registered charitable, non-profit organization founded by Chaplain James Olubunmi Fabunmi. Our mission is to empower and uplift communities by addressing the challenges faced by youths, families, and underserved populations through education, skills development, and healthcare initiatives. We strive to foster unity, resilience, and sustainable growth, creating a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Life is a constant journey of overcoming challenges, and it is through these struggles that we grow and develop. As Albert Einstein once said, “Our lives are built on the labor of others”—a truth that inspires us to give back tirelessly. The Able God Foundation serves as a catalyst for change, providing scholarships to bright but underprivileged students at primary, secondary, and tertiary levels, equipping youths and adults with entrepreneurial skills through our Skills Acquisition Program, and offering free medical outreach to widows, orphans, and vulnerable communities.

Our initiatives, such as the BACK TO BASICS (BTB) program, help families facing financial challenges meet educational costs, while mentorship programs guide youths toward productive futures. In early 2020 alone, we trained over 250 beneficiaries in vocational skills, enabling financial independence just before the COVID-19 lockdown. These efforts continue to transform lives, bridging gaps in education, healthcare, and economic opportunity.

At the heart of our work is the belief that God’s gifts are meant to be shared. Through collaborative efforts, we empower communities to rise above limitations and build brighter futures. Join us in turning compassion into action—together, we can create lasting change, one life at a time.

About Our Leader

Chaplain Dr. Olubunmi James Fabunmi is a preacher, teacher, and globally licensed prophet and evangelist under the Christ Apostolic Church Mission. With a passion for spiritual growth, leadership, and humanitarian service, he has dedicated his life to empowering communities and transforming lives.

Dr. Fabunmi has served as a National Director of Evangelism with the United Pentecostal Church International (UPCI) in Nigeria and is the Senior Pastor of Christ Apostolic Church Oneness. As an educator and missionary, he is committed to raising leaders and fostering development through education, skill acquisition, and spiritual mentorship. Presently, he is the president of Apostolic Alliance Fellowship of Nigeria (A.A.F.N). Married to Pastor Mrs. M. A Fabunmi and blessed with both biological & Spiritual children.
