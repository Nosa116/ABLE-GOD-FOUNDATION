I have been a proud beneficiary of Able God Foundation since 2006, after attending one of Chaplain Dr. J.O. Fabunmi’s Church Outreach Programs in Benin, Edo State, in 2005.

Through the foundation’s generosity, I received an educational scholarship from JSS1 to SS3 in Benin, which paved the way for my higher education. I later gained admission into Adeniran Ogunsanya College of Education, Ijanikin, Lagos, where I graduated before proceeding to Lagos State University, earning a First-Class B.Sc. (Hons.) degree. I successfully completed my NYSC in October 2019.

I am deeply grateful to Able God Foundation—a God-sent agent of hope that transforms lives and restores destinies. I encourage every youth, regardless of background or ethnicity, to embrace the opportunities provided by this incredible organization. Instead of turning to crime or negative influences, take advantage of Able God Foundation’s programs to build a brighter future.

God bless Able God Foundation!

Airehenbuwa Joy

Beneficiary, Benin

I glorify the name of the Most High God for the opportunity and love extended to me through Able God Foundation. Looking back, it feels like just yesterday when my journey began. Able God Foundation transformed my life, and today, I return all glory to God for His mercy, favor, and grace.

In 2005, I started Able God Nursery and Primary School without hope, feeling like a miserable orphan, but God worked through Able God Foundation to give me hope and a future.

Today, I stand as a university graduate, and I urge anyone feeling helpless or hopeless—just as I once did—not to give up. Able God Foundation is a place where lives and destinies are nurtured. Come, embrace the opportunities here, and let God lead your path to success.

I am 100% certain that, like every other beneficiary, you will also have a great testimony to share with the world.

Thank you, Able God Foundation!

Aberejo O.B

Beneficiary, Lagos
